Overview of J-to-A

Our Mission:

J-to-A is a nonprofit that supports Japanese people in Japan and abroad through mental health awareness, advocacy, and community care. The Princeton branch furthers that mission through math and English tutoring, fundraising, and outreach for Japanese immigrant students and families adjusting to life in the United States.
